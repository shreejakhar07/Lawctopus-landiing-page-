Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered

Who is this course for?

Law students, young lawyers, in-house professionals, company secretaries, and anyone who wants to become a contract drafting specialist. If you can read and write English, you can do this course — no prior drafting experience is needed.

Are the sessions live? What if I miss one?

Yes — all 54 sessions are taught live by practicing corporate lawyers. Every live session is recorded, and you receive lifetime access to all recordings and course materials, so missing a session never means missing content.

How are assignments evaluated?

Faculty evaluate your work at five levels — covering structure, legal accuracy, language, commercial sense, and clarity. You receive written feedback on each draft, and your 10 polished contracts become your Upwork portfolio.

What is the freelancing component?

The final module is dedicated to freelancing: building an Upwork profile, crafting winning proposals, pricing services, managing clients, and using Fiverr and LinkedIn. Sessions are led by a top-rated Upwork contract expert who also provides direct project opportunities to participants.

What certificates do I receive?

Every learner who completes the course receives a Completion Certificate from Lawctopus Law School. Top performers are additionally awarded a Merit Certificate. Both are issued digitally and can be shared on LinkedIn or your CV.
